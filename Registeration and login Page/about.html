It was our vision that all newly-weds no longer need to

go through the hassle of standing in lines, going themselves

and signing paperwork,etc. to get themselves registered as married,

this website does that for them.

Users need to first register themselves on this platform after which

a congratulatory certificate will be generated containing all important

information about the lovebirds and nothing but good-wishes from our side.

Now the couples don't need to waste their time, rather spend it lovingly with each other.

With this vision in mind, this website was born with the most utmost sincerity and love.
